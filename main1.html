Bulacan Campus Admission

The Bestlink College of the Philippines provide a high-quality educational experience as well as a loving environment that promotes keen learning abilities. We are proud of our personnel and the work they accomplish, but we are even prouder of the students with whom we interact on a daily basis.


We have worked hard at The Bestlink College of the Philippines to make our admissions process as straightforward as possible so that all potential parents can have a stress-free application experience.


Step 1: Inquire About admission.

  • Visit the school admissions office or submit the online Enquiry Form from this link. Our counselor will contact you to respond to your questions and get the information needed.

Step 2: Registration

  • Students are encouraged to visit the school's Admissions Office, complete the registration form, and purchase a copy of the school prospectus. Please include one recent passport-size photo. Students can also download the application form here (give the link). Please print the completed form, fill in the details, and return it to the school in hard copy.

Step 3: Submitting the form and Requirements

  • The following documents need to be submitted along with the Registration Form
